Delete Your Shafio Account

You can delete your account and all associated data directly in the Shafio app.

How to delete your account

  1. Open the Shafio app on your device.
  2. Tap the Settings icon.
  3. Scroll to Delete Account and tap it.
  4. Confirm deletion in the dialog.

What gets deleted

  • Your sign-in credentials and device registration.
  • All pantry items, recipes, and usage data stored on your device.
  • Your usage quota record on our servers.

What is retained

No personal data (name, email, or payment info) is stored on our servers. Subscription billing records are managed by Apple App Store or Google Play and are subject to their respective retention policies.
